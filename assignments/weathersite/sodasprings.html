Soda Springs City Events

Summer Recreational Sports

Author: Dwight Shrute

Soda Springs is preparing for their annual summer recreational sports. There are so many options available for the community to stay involved in town events. Visit our website www.sodaspringsid.com/recreation/recreational_sports/index.php to see a schedule of the games and the type of sports available to sign up for.
We have activities for children as young as 3 years old but the sports aren’t only for the kids either! We have adult leagues available for men’s basketball, adult flag football and volleyball so the whole family can be involved.
